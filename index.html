YOUR MOVE
This Weekend. 4 Steps.
A field guide for building your first thing with AI ~1 weekend · free tools only
🔍 1. Research: Saturday Morning (~1 hr)
Pick one problem you actually care about. Use Perplexity to find what already exists and who's complaining. Ask: "What do people keep wishing existed?" That's your idea. Don't overthink it. You just need one curiosity worth a weekend.
🛠 2. Build: Saturday Afternoon (2–4 hrs)
Describe what you want in plain English. Use v0.dev, Bolt.new, or Lovable. No code needed to start. Prompt: "Build a simple tool that does [X]. Keep it basic and functional." Iterate until it works. You will be amazed what ships in an afternoon.
🚀 3. Ship: Sunday (~30 min)
Deploy on Vercel or Netlify. Both are free and one-click. Get a real URL. Then post it: LinkedIn, X, anywhere. Once it's live, the work becomes real. One person using your thing changes how you see yourself.
⚡ 4. Level Up: Ongoing
After the first version, go deeper with Cursor or Claude Code. The first build changes your identity a little. The second starts the habit. Free resources, real examples, and prompts included.
